ABOUT US

YS Study Focus is a trusted and officially authorized student placement agency, recognized by top Malaysian universities. Since our founding in 2024 by Sabrina Jannat Suchana, we’ve become a reliable name for international student services — built on transparency, care, and results.

We proudly maintain official partnerships with 6 leading universities and have successfully processed over 240 student applications. Our students have gained admission to prestigious institutions including:
City University, Alfa University, UCMI, UPM, King’s University, Asia e University, INTI International University, SEGi University, and many more.

Today, our team of 8 dedicated agents is actively guiding students from start to finish — ensuring a smooth transition into Malaysian universities. From choosing the right program to visa and document support, YS Study Focus is with you every step of the way.

Our mission is simple: to open doors for students through quality education, genuine guidance, and dependable support.
